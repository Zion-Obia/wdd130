Choose Your Adventure

We offer a variety of trips to suit every adventurer, from serene floats to wild rapids. Find the perfect trip for you and your group below. All trips include expert guides, safety gear, and lunch.

Book Your Trip Now!

Sunset Float (Half-Day)

Perfect for families and first-timers. Enjoy a gentle float down a scenic stretch of the river as the sun sets. This trip is all about relaxation and enjoying the beautiful scenery. Suitable for all ages.

Canyon Run (Full-Day)

Our most popular trip! Navigate through exciting Class III and IV rapids in a stunning canyon. This full-day adventure is packed with thrills, teamwork, and incredible views. Previous experience is recommended but not required.

The Gauntlet (Full-Day)

For the experienced thrill-seeker. This trip tackles challenging Class V rapids and requires physical fitness and confidence in the water. A true test of skill and nerve, guided by our most experienced staff.
